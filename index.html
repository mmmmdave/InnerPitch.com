Fake personalization doesn't work

“Hi Dave, I see you went to Syracuse and you’re the VP of Marketing.” Terrific — you did surface-level web scraping. But you didn’t actually connect.

People don’t respond based on you. They respond based on themselves — their internal motivations, identity, status, pride, fears.
